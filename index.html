Got questions?
Frequently asked
📍 Where does PlateRun deliver right now?
+
We currently deliver within Johar Town, Lahore. We're actively expanding — DHA, Gulberg, Model Town, and Wapda Town coming very soon. Enter your area on the homepage to check availability.
⏱️ How long does delivery actually take?
+
Average delivery time is 25–45 minutes. Restaurant prep is usually 15–25 minutes plus rider travel. You'll see a real-time estimate on your order confirmation and WhatsApp receipt.
💵 What payment methods are accepted?
+
Cash on Delivery (COD), JazzCash, and EasyPaisa. No credit card required. JazzCash/EasyPaisa instructions are sent in your WhatsApp receipt.
📱 Do I need to download an app?
+
No app needed! PlateRun works entirely in your browser on any phone, tablet, or computer. Your account and order history are saved automatically.
💬 How does the WhatsApp receipt work?
+
When you place an order, a detailed receipt is sent straight to your WhatsApp — your name, order items, total, delivery address, and payment method. You'll also receive updates when your order is confirmed, prepared, and when the rider picks it up.
❌ What if my order is wrong or late?
+
Contact us via WhatsApp at 0307-6064194. For wrong orders we'll arrange a replacement or refund. For delays beyond 60 minutes, you'll get Rs. 100 credit on your next order.
🍽️ I'm a restaurant owner — how do I join?
+
WhatsApp us on 0307-6064194 or email rshazab91@gmail.com. Your first month is completely commission-free. We can have you live within 24 hours — menu setup and onboarding at zero cost.
🔐 How is my personal information stored?
+
Your name, email, phone and address are stored securely and only used to process and deliver your orders. We never sell your data. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
