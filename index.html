Got questions?
Frequently asked
Everything you need to know before your first order.
📍 Where does PlateRun deliver right now?
+
We currently deliver within Johar Town, Lahore. We're actively expanding — DHA, Gulberg, Model Town, and Wapda Town are coming very soon. Enter your area on the homepage to check availability.
⏱️ How long does delivery actually take?
+
Average delivery time is 25–45 minutes depending on the restaurant and your distance. Restaurant preparation time is usually 15–25 minutes, plus rider travel time. You'll see a real-time estimate on your order confirmation.
💵 What payment methods are accepted?
+
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), JazzCash, and EasyPaisa. Pay whichever way is easiest for you — no credit card required. When paying via JazzCash/EasyPaisa, payment instructions are sent to your WhatsApp.
📱 Do I need to download an app?
+
No app download needed! PlateRun works entirely in your browser on any phone, tablet, or computer. Just open platerun.pk and order. Your account and order history are saved automatically.
🔁 How does order tracking work?
+
You'll receive WhatsApp notifications at every stage: when your order is received, when the restaurant confirms it, when your rider picks it up, and an estimated arrival time. You can also check your order status live in your account dashboard.
❌ What if my order is wrong or late?
+
Contact us immediately via WhatsApp support (link in footer). For wrong orders, we'll either arrange a replacement delivery or a full refund. For delays beyond 60 minutes, you're entitled to a Rs. 100 credit on your next order. We take responsibility seriously.
🔐 How is my personal information stored?
+
Your name, email, phone, and address are stored securely and only used to process and deliver your orders. We never sell your data to third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.
🍽️ I'm a restaurant owner — how do I join?
+
Email us at partners@platerun.pk or WhatsApp us on 0309-1116789. Your first month is completely commission-free. We can have you live within 24 hours — we'll handle menu photography, setup, and onboarding at no cost to you.
